FAQ

The questions everyone asks first.

Does it actually publish to my site?
Yes. Connect your CMS once (WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, Ghost, Sanity, or headless via webhook) and the agent ships drafts or live posts on the cadence you pick. You can require human approval per post if you want.
How is this different from Ahrefs or Semrush?
Those are dashboards — they tell you what to do. AutoSEO is an agent — it actually does it. Research, write, publish, internally link, and rewrite what slips. You review outcomes, not todos.
What is GEO and why does it matter?
Generative Engine Optimization: showing up as the cited, trusted answer when people ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Google AI, or Copilot. A growing share of buying research never touches a blue link — GEO is how you stay visible there.
Will Google penalize AI-written content?
Google's stance is helpful, accurate, human-reviewable content — regardless of how it's made. Every piece is fact-checked, cites sources, follows E-E-A-T, and is tuned to your brand voice. We also track rankings daily and rewrite anything that underperforms.
Can I keep a human in the loop?
Yes. Default is human-gated publish. You can also flip destructive actions (meta rewrites on hero pages, outreach emails, etc.) to require explicit approval and keep low-risk actions automatic.
What do I need to get started?
A domain and 90 seconds. Paste your URL — the agent crawls, learns your brand, proposes a plan, and asks for your objective. You approve; it starts.
